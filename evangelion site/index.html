NEON GENESIS EVANGELION

Neon Genesis Evangelion is a Japanese anime and manga series that was first aired back in 1995. The story is set in a 2015 Tokyo, after a global cataclysm has wiped out most of humanity. The remaining survivors are threatened by powerful creatures known as Angels, and the only hope for saving their world are the Evangelion units, which are giant mechas piloted by a select group of teenagers.

There are three main characters of the series: Shinji, Asuka, and Rei. They are junior high students who are chosen by NERV to command their respective mechas and fight the Angels.
